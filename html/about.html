Our Story

Founded in 2010, Triz Cuts began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional grooming services in an environment that honors the tradition of barbering while embracing modern techniques and styles.

What started as a small two-chair shop has grown into a premier destination for men's grooming in the city. Our success is built on our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and the relationships we build with our clients.
