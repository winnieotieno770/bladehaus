Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

+

While we do accept walk-ins when available, we highly recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get your preferred time slot and barber.

How long does a typical haircut take?

+

A standard haircut typically takes 30-45 minutes. Services like the Haircut & Beard Combo or Hot Towel Shave may take up to an hour.

What forms of payment do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do you offer gift cards?

+

Yes, we offer gift cards in any denomination. They can be purchased in-store or by calling us.
